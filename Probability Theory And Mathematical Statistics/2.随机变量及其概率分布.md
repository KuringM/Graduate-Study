## 连续型随机变量

![C5977652-A09E-45AC-B163-F4D608AC659B.jpeg](./Accessory/第二章\ 随机变量及其概率分布/C5977652-A09E-45AC-B163-F4D608AC659B.jpeg)

- $P\{x_1<X\le x_2 \}=F(x_2)-F(x_1)$的推导
    
    $$\begin{aligned}
    &令A表示X \le x_2,B表 \le x_1,B \subset A\\
    &P\{x_1< X \le x2\}\\
    &=P\{A \bar{B}\}\\
    &=P\{A-B\}\\
    &=P\{A\}-P\{AB\}\\
    &=P\{A\}-P\{B\}\\
    &=F(x_2)-F(x_1)
    \end{aligned} $$
