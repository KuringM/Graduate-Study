### 瑕积分判别定理常用结论
**常用结论:** 
$$\begin{aligned}
&\int_a^{b}\frac{1}{(x-a)^p}\mathrm{d}x
\left\{\begin{array}{ll}
p<1, 收敛\\
p\ge 1, 发散
\end{array}\right.\\
&\int_a^{b}\frac{1}{(b-x)^p}\mathrm{d}x
\left\{\begin{array}{ll}
p<1, 收敛\\
p\ge 1, 发散
\end{array}\right.\\

证明如下:\\

&\int_{a}^{b}\frac{{}\mathrm{d}x}{(x-a)^p}
\left \{ \begin{array}{ll} 
&\frac{(x-a)^{-p+1}}{-p+1}\bigg|_{a}^{b}
\left \{ \begin{array}{ll} 
&-p+1>0 \quad \frac{(b-a)^{-p+1}}{-p+1},\qquad p<1\\
&-p+1<0 \quad +∞,\qquad p>1\\
\end{array}\right. \\
&ln(x-a) \bigg|_{a}^{b}=+∞,\qquad p=1 
\end{array}\right. \\
&\int_{a}^{b}\frac{{}\mathrm{d}x}{(x-a)^p}
\left \{ \begin{array}{ll} 
p<1,收敛\\
p \ge 1,发散\\
\end{array}\right.
&\end{aligned} $$
