## 几个重要不等式

$$\begin{aligned} 
&\sin x < x < \tan x,\quad x\in(0,\frac{\pi}{2})\\ 
&\frac{x}{1+x} < \ln (1+x) < x,\quad x\in(0,+∞)\\ 
&x\le e^x -1\le xe^x\\ 
&2ab\le a^2+b^2\\ 
&\sqrt[3]{abc}\leq \frac{a+b+c}{3}\\ &|a-b|\geq \mid |a|-|b|\mid\\ 
&\mid a\pm b\mid \le |a|+|b|\\
& |f(a)|+|f(b)|\le \ 2max(|f(a)|,|f(b)|)
\end{aligned}$$
