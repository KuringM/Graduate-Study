## 阶乘与双阶乘

### 阶乘

$$
n! = 1\cdot 2\cdot 3\cdots n, 规定0! =1
$$

### 双阶乘

- 双阶乘不是阶乘的阶乘
- $(6)!! = 6\times 4\times 2$<br>
- $(5)!! = 5\times 3\times 1$

$$
\begin{aligned}
	& n!!= \prod\limits_{k=0}^{[\frac{n}{2}]-1} (n-2k)=n(n-2)(n-4)\cdots
\end{aligned}
$$

1. $(2n)!! = 2 \cdot 4\cdot 6 \cdots (2n)= 2^n \cdot n!$
2. $(2n-1)!! = 1 \cdot 3\cdot 5 \cdots (2n-1)$
