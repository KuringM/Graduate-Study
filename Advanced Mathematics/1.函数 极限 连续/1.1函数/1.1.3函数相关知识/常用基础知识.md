当...有: when...there  
设: let, assume  
令: let  
解: soln, solution, pf, proof  
因为...所以: since...(so)  
即: i.e. (id est)  
假设: suppose  
由假设: by suppose  
当且仅当: iff(if and only if)  
同理: similarly  
此外: moreover  
且: and  
这表明: This implies that  
综上所述: Summarizing  
得出...结论: We conclude that ...  
证明完毕: Q.E.D.(quod erat demonstrandum)  
从而: thereby  
满足: meet.
