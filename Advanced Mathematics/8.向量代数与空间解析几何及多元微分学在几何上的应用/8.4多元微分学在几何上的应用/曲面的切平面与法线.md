## 1. 曲面$F(x, y, z)=0$的切平面与法线

$$
\begin{aligned}
	& 曲面隐式方程: F(x, y, z)=0\\
	& 曲面显式方程: z= f(x,y)\\
	& 曲面的偏导数在该点连续且不同时为0
\end{aligned}
$$

### 曲线在$(x, y, z)$处的法向量

$$
\begin{aligned}
	& \vec{n}=(F'_x, F'_y, F'_{z})\\
	& \vec{n}=(f'_x, f'_{y}, -1)
\end{aligned}
$$

### 曲线在$(x, y, z)$处的切平面

$$
F_x'(x-x_0)+F_y'(y-y_0)+F_z'(z-z_0)=0
$$

### 曲线在$(x, y, z)$处的法线

$$
\begin{aligned}
	& \frac{x-x_0}{F_x'} =\frac{y-y_0}{F_y'} =\frac{z-z_0}{F_z'}\\
	& \frac{x-x_0}{f_x'} =\frac{y-y_0}{f_y'} =\frac{z-z_0}{-1}\\
\end{aligned}
$$
