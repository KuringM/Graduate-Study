## 解

$$
\begin{aligned}
	& C\\\\
	& y=\frac{x^2+x}{x^2-1}= \frac{x}{x-1}, x\not= \pm 1\\
	& x\to \infty, y=1(水平渐近线)\\
	& x\to 1, y\to \infty(垂直渐近线)\\
	& x\to -1, y\to \frac{1}{2}\\
	& \frac{y}{x}= \frac{1}{x-1}, x\to \infty, y\to \infty(无斜渐近线)
\end{aligned}
$$
