# 思

- 复合函数求导数

# 解

$$
\begin{aligned}
	& \frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}x}\bigg|_{x=e}= \frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}u}\bigg|_{u=\frac{1}{2}}\frac{\mathrm{d}u}{\mathrm{d}x}\bigg|_{x=e}
	=f'(\frac{1}{2})f'(e)= 2 \frac{1}{2e}= \frac{1}{e}
\end{aligned}
$$
