# 思

- 无穷小
- 齐次线性微分方程

# 解

$$
\begin{aligned}
	& \lim\limits_{\Delta x\to 0} \frac{f(x+ \Delta x)-f(x)}{\Delta x}= \lim\limits_{\Delta x\to 0} [2xf(x)+\frac{o(\Delta x)}{\Delta x}]= 2xf(x)\\
	& \therefore f'(x)= 2xf(x)\\
	& \frac{\mathrm{d}f(x)}{f(x)}= 2x\mathrm{d}x\\
	& f(x)= C e^{x^2}= 2 e^{x^2} \quad f(0)= 2\\
	& f(1)= 2e
\end{aligned}
$$
