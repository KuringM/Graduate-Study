## 解
$$
\begin{aligned}
	& 选C.\\
	&
	f(x)=
	\left\{
	\begin{array}{ll}
	4x^3, x>0\\
	2x^3, x<0
	\end{array}
	\right.
	\newline
	&
	f'(x)=
	\left\{
	\begin{array}{ll}
	12x^2, x>0\\
	6x^2, x<0
	\end{array}
	\right.
	\newline
	&
	f''(x)=
	\left\{
	\begin{array}{ll}
	24x, x>0\\
	12x, x<0
	\end{array}
	\right.
	\newline
	& f'(0)=f''(0)=0,\\
	& f_-'''(0)=12\not =f_+'''(0)=24
\end{aligned}
$$
