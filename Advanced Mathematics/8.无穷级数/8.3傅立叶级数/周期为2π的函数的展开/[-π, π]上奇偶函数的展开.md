### $[-\pi, \pi]$上奇偶函数的展开
#### f(x)为奇函数
$$\begin{aligned}
&a_n=0, &\qquad n=0, 1, 2,
\cdots, \\
&b_n=\frac{2}{\pi}\int_{0}^{\pi}f(x)\sin nx \mathrm{d}x, &\qquad n=1, 2, \cdots.\\
\end{aligned}$$

#### f(x)为偶函数
$$\begin{aligned}
a_n&=\frac{2}{\pi}\int_{0}^{\pi}f(x)\cos nx \mathrm{d}x, &\qquad n=0, 1, 2,
\cdots, \\
b_n&=0, &\qquad n=1, 2, \cdots.\\
\end{aligned}$$
