1. $|A^T|=|A|$$(\textcolor{green}{D=D^T}$ $\textcolor{green}{D=D^T}$ )
2. 互换两行(列),值变号($\textcolor{green}{|E(i,j)|=-1}$)
    1. 两行(列)相同或成比例,$D=0$
3. $|kA|=k^n|A|$($\textcolor{green}{|E(i(k))|=k}$)
    1. 某行()列全为0,$D=0$
    2. 两行(列)相同或成比例,$D=0$
    3. 某行(列)的k的倍可以单独提出来
4. 拆开法

$$\begin{vmatrix}
a_1+b_1 & a_2+b_2 & a_3+b_3 \\
c1 & c2 & c3 \\
d1 & d2 & d3 
\end{vmatrix}=
\begin{vmatrix}
a_1 & a_2 & a_3 \\
c_1 & c_2 & c_3 \\
d_1 & d_2 & d_3 
\end{vmatrix}+
\begin{vmatrix}
b_1 & b_2 & b_3 \\
c_1 & c_2 & c_3 \\
d_1 & d_2 & d_3 
\end{vmatrix}$$
