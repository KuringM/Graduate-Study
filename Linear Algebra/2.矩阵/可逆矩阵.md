## 可逆矩阵
$$\begin{aligned}
& 可逆矩阵(\textcolor{green}{非奇异矩阵}, 方阵)\ AB=BA=E\quad , 则A^{-1}=B\\
& 定理:\\
& \qquad (1)\ A可逆, A^{-1}唯一\\
& \qquad (2)\ A可逆 \Leftrightarrow |A|\not=0 \textcolor{red}{\Leftrightarrow r(A)=n\Leftrightarrow A线性无关}\\
& \qquad (3)\ A, B为方阵, AB=E, 则BA=E\\
\end{aligned}$$
